About

What Authentica is

Authentica is a neutral guide to trust, provenance, and verification in domains where counterfeits and misattribution are common and the downside is real.

Mission

When authenticity becomes a marketing claim, people lose money, time, and confidence. Authentica exists to clarify what “proof” looks like, why common verification approaches fail, and where to find services that can help.

What we do

  • Publish evergreen explainers on authenticity, provenance, and verification methods.
  • Maintain a small, categorized directory of services and tools for discovery.
  • Provide clear disclosure so readers understand commercial relationships.

What we do not do

  • We do not authenticate items, inspect goods, or issue certificates.
  • We do not guarantee outcomes or take responsibility for third-party services.
  • We do not operate as a marketplace or escrow agent.
Extensible by design: Authentica is category-agnostic and can expand into additional verticals where trust and provenance matter (luxury, art, collectibles, digital provenance, and beyond).
