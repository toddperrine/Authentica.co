About

What Authentica is

Authentica is a small reference site focused on authentication and identity: MFA, SSO, IAM/CIAM, passkeys, passwordless, and practical access policy. The goal is clarity over hype.

What we do

  • Explain definitions and tradeoffs (plain language, no marketing).
  • Publish rollout checklists and “what breaks” guidance.
  • Provide vendor categories and evaluation rubrics.

What we do not do

  • Provide security guarantees, audits, or compliance certifications.
  • Offer incident response, pen testing, or formal risk assessments.
  • Endorse a “best vendor”—fit depends on context.

Editorial stance

The content is vendor-aware and pragmatic. When affiliate or referral relationships exist, they are disclosed. See Disclosure.
