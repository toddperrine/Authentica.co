About

What Authentica is

Authentica is a small reference site focused on authentication and identity: MFA, SSO, IAM, CIAM, passkeys, and the tradeoffs that matter in real deployments.

What this site is for

  • Clarify terminology (so teams stop talking past each other).
  • Highlight failure modes (what breaks after rollout).
  • Map vendor categories and decision criteria.

What this site is not

Authentica does not sell authentication software, perform audits, or provide security consulting. It exists to clarify terms, tradeoffs, and vendor categories so teams can make better decisions.
