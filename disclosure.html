Disclosure

Disclosure & editorial independence

Authentica aims to be vendor-neutral. This page explains how outbound links work and what “neutral” means in practice.

Referral links

Some outbound links on Authentica may be referral or affiliate links. If you click a referral link and later purchase, Authentica may receive compensation from the vendor at no additional cost to you.

  • Referral links are optional and do not affect which vendor categories are recommended.
  • We do not accept payment in exchange for a specific ranking or recommendation.
  • When we mention vendors, we aim to list alternatives and explain tradeoffs.

What “vendor-neutral” means here

  • We start with failure modes (how accounts actually get compromised), then map controls to those risks.
  • We separate vendor category guidance (e.g., MFA vs PAM) from product selection.
  • We prioritize operational reality: recovery, helpdesk reset, break-glass, outages, and exceptions.
