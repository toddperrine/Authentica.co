If you only do three things

  1. Enforce MFA with phishing-resistant methods for privileged actions (prefer passkeys or FIDO2 where possible).
  2. Adopt SSO where it reduces password sprawl and centralizes policy (start with your highest-risk apps).
  3. Use conditional access and device posture to reduce session hijacking and limit blast radius.

Practical framing

Buy platforms after you’ve named the failure mode you’re preventing: takeover, reset abuse, session hijack, privilege escalation, or outage risk.
