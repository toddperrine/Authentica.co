Why this matters now

Counterfeits are more convincing, provenance is easier to forge, and AI can fabricate supporting evidence at scale. When the stakes are real, “looks right” is not a standard.

Where authentication breaks

Paper certificates are copied, databases are incomplete, and conflicts of interest are common. Many verification systems are theater: persuasive, but not durable under scrutiny.

What builds trust

Trust is constructed from provenance, chain of custody, independent inspection, and transparent methods—often with a hybrid of human judgment and specialized tools.
