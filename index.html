If you only do three things

  • Enforce MFA with phishing-resistant methods for privileged actions (prefer passkeys or FIDO2 where possible).
  • Adopt SSO where it reduces password sprawl and centralizes policy (start with your highest-risk apps).
  • Use conditional access and device posture to reduce session hijacking and limit blast radius.
