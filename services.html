Evaluation rubric (use this on every vendor call)

  • Threat model: phishing, MFA fatigue, SIM swap, token/session theft, insider risk.
  • Assurance: can you enforce phishing-resistant methods for high-risk actions?
  • Lifecycle: joiner/mover/leaver automation; SCIM; HRIS integration.
  • Policy depth: conditional access, device posture, geo/risk signals, step-up auth.
  • UX: enrollment, recovery, helpdesk flows, admin experience.
  • Cost drivers: MAUs, apps, integrations, feature tiers, support plans.
