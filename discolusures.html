Disclosure & ethics

Authentica is designed to be a neutral guide. Commercial relationships exist in the form of referral links and, in some cases, paid placement. This page explains how that works.

Affiliate / referral links

Some outbound links may include tracking parameters that identify Authentica as a referral source. If a reader clicks and completes a qualifying action (purchase, inquiry, or transaction), Authentica may earn a fee.

Paid listings and featured placement

Some providers may pay for featured placement. Featured placement does not imply endorsement. Listings are intended for discovery; readers should perform independent diligence.

Sponsored content

Sponsored content, if published, will be clearly labeled as sponsored. Authentica does not accept undisclosed sponsorships.

Editorial independence

  • Authentica does not authenticate items and does not inspect goods.
  • Authentica does not guarantee outcomes of third-party services.
  • Where practical, listings focus on method clarity and appropriate use cases rather than marketing claims.

Contact

Providers may request inclusion or featured placement: partners@authentica.co.

Note: This site is intentionally lightweight and portable. Monetization exists to support maintenance and validate commercial intent, not to maximize ad yield.
